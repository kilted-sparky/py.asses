=====Calculator HELP=====

The Calculator can be used to help you with multiple Mathematical queries.

If you choose option 1 then you will be able to enter 2 numbers and the calculator 
will add those together and give you the answer.

If you choose option 2 then the calculator will take the 2 numbers entered and 
subtract the first from the second number entered and then give you the answer.

If you choose option 3 the calculator will multiply the first number by the second number
and give you the answer.

If you choose option 4 then the calculator will divide the first number entered by the second
and give you the answer.

Option 5 will return you to the Main Menu.

Once you have completed the mathematical sum it will ask if you would like to do another
you can choose one of the 5 options again.
